From childhood I have always been inspired by the beauty of flowering plants, from botanical gardens to the voluptuous blossoms of Bougainvillea covering our home. My first recollections of being enamoured by the beauty of blossoms are from kindergarten in the Netherlands... Sitting on lawns stringing meadow daisy chains and dressing up as a flower queen are memories still fresh in my minds eye.

I moved to the United States in my late teens, and graduated from art school with a major in Design. I worked retail with the Laura Ashley Group, and loved seeing the floral patterns they are famous for. While raising two sons I engaged in many creative activities,including gardening, specifically working with flowers, blooming shrubs and trees.

With my children raised, I have started exploring my life-long goal of painting, From that time, I have been part of a group of local painters. My work is on display in several locations, as well as also organized private group and solo exhibits.
